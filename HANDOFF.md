CreditMemo is the AR negative — needs an "apply to invoice" path mirroring `qb_payment_apply`. PurchaseOrder is the Bill twin (vendor side). 4. **Watch for entity-specific quirks**:
   - **SalesReceipt** — has Subtotal/SalesTaxTotal/TotalAmount but no BalanceRemaining (cash sale, instantly closed). Posts to a deposit account, not AR. `computeTotals` may need a new branch for SalesReceipt.

   - **JournalEntry** — DebitLine + CreditLine; sum(debits) === sum(credits) is a hard QB invariant.
